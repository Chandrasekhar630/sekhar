Narendra Modi

Early Life of Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi was born on 17 September 1950 to a Gujarati Hindu family of OBC background in Vadnagar, Mehsana district, Bombay State (present-day Gujarat). He was the third of six children born to Damodardas Mulchand Modi and Hiraben Modi.

Birth

17 September 1950 (Age 74), Vadnagar, Gujarat, India.

Ancestry and Self-Identity

Modi rose to prominence leading the BJP to victory in the 2014 Indian general election. His tenure has been marked by significant reforms, international diplomacy, and major events like demonetization, the Goods and Services Tax, revoking Article 370, and handling the COVID-19 pandemic. In 2024, his party formed a coalition government.

Education and Training

Modi earned a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in political science from Delhi University and a Master of Arts (MA) in political science from Gujarat University as a distance learning student.
